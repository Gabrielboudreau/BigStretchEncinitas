Meet BIG Stretch.

Helping Encinitas move, feel, and perform at their best—one stretch at a time.

BIG Stretch was created to help our Encinitas community move, feel, and perform at their best. Based inside Anytime Fitness Encinitas, we bring a unique blend of expert-assisted stretching, wellness experience, and personalized attention to every client.

Our team of trained stretch practitioners (Flexologists) uses proven techniques to improve flexibility, relieve muscle tension, and enhance overall well-being. Whether you’re training for a sport, recovering from a workout, or just want to feel better in your body, we’re here to help.
