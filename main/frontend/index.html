The Science of Stretching – Why Assisted Stretching is a Game-Changer.

Stretching is more than just a warm-up. It’s an essential component of recovery, injury prevention, and maintaining a healthy body. Our assisted stretching sessions go beyond what you can do on your own, helping to:

  • Improve Flexibility and Mobility: Increase your range of motion and reduce stiffness.
  • Boost Recovery: Release tension, flush out lactic acid, and speed up healing.
  • Prevent Injury: Regular stretching reduces the risk of strains and joint pain.
  • Relieve Pain and Stress: Target trouble spots to help with back pain, tight hips, or stress.
